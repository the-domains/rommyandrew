Poetry

The full moon blooms brightly with generous fulfillment as the earth worm stirs in tender nourishment

A familiar dialect remembers in our hearts as it swiftly swirls unhindered on our perfect lips

Mega inspiration from a nomadic 3-day trip down the PCH with a great friend. This particular photo is from a little beach cove in Big Sur.

Rommy Andrew is a Washington, DC-based singer/songwriter and poet devoted to sharing his passion for music and creating loving spaces to share his work.
